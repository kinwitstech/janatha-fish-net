Fishing Nets - Mono Filament

Nylon Mono-filament Fishnet

A Nylon Mono-filament Fishnet is made from a single, continuous strand of high-quality nylon, creating a strong and durable mesh. Unlike multifilament nets, it features a smooth, thick filament with fewer knots, offering minimal drag and resistance for efficient, precision fishing in commercial settings.

Why Choose Nylon Mono-filament Fishnets?

Nylon Mono-filament Fishnets offer precision with low visibility, durability against wear, UV, and saltwater, and reduced drag for faster deployment. Fewer knots minimize tangling, enhancing efficiency and performance in demanding conditions.

Gillnetting: Selective, minimal harm.
Trawling: Low drag, efficient.
Seine Fishing: Precision, minimal disturbance.
Commercial Fishing: High-volume, durable.
Targeting Wary Species: Catches elusive fish.

Key Features

Continuous Nylon Strand

Single, durable filament for enhanced strength.

Smooth Surface

Reduces water resistance and drag for better performance

Low Visibility

Ideal for catching wary fish with minimal detection.

Fewer Knots

Minimizes entanglements, improving ease of use.

UV & Saltwater Resistant

Ensures durability in harsh environments.
