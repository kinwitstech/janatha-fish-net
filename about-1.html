Chairman’s Message
Pioneering the Future of Fishing

As we look to the future, Janatha Fishnets is committed to leading the way in innovation, sustainability, and empowerment. We are not just keeping pace with change—we’re driving it. Through cutting-edge technology and eco-conscious solutions, we’re redefining what it means to fish responsibly, efficiently, and ethically. Our dedication to empowering communities, particularly women, continues to be a cornerstone of our success, with over 70% of our workforce being driven by passion, excellence, and purpose. This strong foundation enables us to push boundaries and build a more sustainable, inclusive future for the fishing industry. As we forge ahead, we’re focused on smarter, cleaner solutions and global partnerships that will set new standards in the industry. The future is here, and together, we’ll continue to shape it. Thank you for your continued trust and support.

Anand C. Kunder

Our Promise

Driven By Innovation, Grounded in Responsibility

Uncompromising Quality

Our rigorous manufacturing process and advanced quality checks, backed by an in-house lab, ensure that every product meets the highest standards of excellence. At Janatha Fishnets, quality is never compromised.



Eco-Conscious Innovation

We are committed to sustainability with eco-friendly practices, including wastewater recycling and air pollution reduction, to minimize our environmental impact. Our efforts focus on creating a greener future for the fishing industry.


Streamlined Operations

The integration of SAP ERP and HRMS has revolutionized our operations, enhancing coordination across departments and enabling real-time tracking, smarter decision-making, and efficient production planning. This technological edge allows us to offer superior service and maintain a competitive advantage.
